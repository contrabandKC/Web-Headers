WE ARE SCE

UMKC School of Computing and Engineering (SCE) is Kansas City’s only accredited school for engineering and computer science. We welcome students from across the nation and around the world to join us in the pursuit of high-quality educational experiences and extracurricular activities that help shape the professionals they become. We can’t wait for you to be part of SCE!
